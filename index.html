Identify failed startups with evidence of initial customer traction, signaling they found a real problem.

Step 2: Diligence & The Access Wedge

Gain access to assets by offering solutions: a clean wind-down for VCs, a soft landing for founders, or a small cash recovery for a worthless asset.

Perform data archaeology on Slack, emails, and support tickets to find the gap between what customers wanted and what the company built.

Acquire assets cheaply and task a lean team to build the product the extracted customer data actually describes.
